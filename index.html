📊 Net Profit Calculation

  • Formula: Projected Sales Revenue minus Sourcing Costs (COGS) minus your Operational Expenses.
  • Scan Revenue: Generated instantly from historical marketplace data parsed through the Gemini 3.5 vision engine.
  • Overhead Liability: Items logged via the 'Add Expense' workflow subtract directly from this dashboard card value.

💡 Sourcing Cost Breakdown

  • Thrift/Bundles: Take the total checkout receipt cost divided evenly by the number of usable items purchased.
  • Unearned/Found Items: Enter $0. If gas or travel overhead was required for pickup, log it down under general operational expenses.
  • Repairs & Cleaning: If dedicated parts or restoration chemicals were bought for this specific unit, add that to the initial value here.
